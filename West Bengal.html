West Bengal:

Food:

Bhapa Ilish is a very popular dish in Bengal. Langcha is a famous sweet
of West Bengal. This is one of those popular fried sweets that India
is famous for. Shaktigarh in the Bardhaman District of West Bengal
is famous for this sweet item.

Vegetation:

The commonly identifiable vegetation types in the dense
Sundarbans mangrove forests are salt water mixed forest,
mangrove scrub, brackish water mixed forest, littoral forest,
wet forest and wet alluvial grass forests.

Climate:

The plains of South Bengal feels hot and humid conditions
with daily maximum temperature around 35-38 °C.
Sometimes it nears or crosses 40 °C. The western highlands experience
a dry summer like northern India.
