Arunachal Pradesh:

Food:

Apong or rice beer made from fermented rice or millet is a popular beverage
in Arunachal Pradesh, as an alcoholic drink. There are different varieties of rice
beer with different flavours. The staple food is rice along with fish, meat (Lukter)
and many green vegetables.

Vegetation:

The vegetation of Arunachal Pradesh falls under four broad climatic categories and
can be classified in five broad forest types with a sixth type of secondary forests.
These are tropical forests, sub tropical forests, pine forests, temperate forests
and alpine forests.

Climate:
