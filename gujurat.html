Gujurat:

Food:

Gujarati cuisine is that of the state of Gujarat, in western India. The typical Gujarati
thali consists of rotli, dal or kadhi, rice, and shaak (a dish made up of several different combinations of
vegetables and spices, which may be either spicy or sweet).

Vegetation:

The main vegetation types of south Gujarat includes teak mixed dry and moist deciduous forest,
mangrove forest, mangrove scrub, riverain forest, ravine thorn forest,
forest plantation, degraded forest, scrub, Prosopis juliflora, grassland and
orchard respectively.

Climate:

The climate of Gujarat involves diverse conditions. The plains of Gujarat are very hot and dry in
summer and cold and dry in winter. Summer is milder in the hilly regions and the coast.
