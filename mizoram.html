Mizoram:

Food:

Rice is the staple food of Mizoram, while Mizos love to add non-vegetarian ingredients in every dish.
Fish, chicken, pork and beef are popular meats among Mizos.
Dishes are cooked in any available oil. Meals tend to be blander with less oil and more
vegetables.

Vegetation:

Tropical semi-evergreen, tropical moist deciduous, subtropical broadleaved hill and subtropical
pine forests are the most common vegetation types found in Mizoram.
Bamboo is common in the state, typically intermixed with other forest vegetation; about 9,245 km2
(44%) of the state's area is bamboo bearing.

Climate:

Climate. Mizoram has a mild climate, being relatively cool in summer 20 to 29 째C (68 to 84 째F)
but progressively warmer, most probably due to climate change, with summer temperatures crossing 30 degrees
Celsius and winter temperatures ranging from 7 to 22 째C (45 to 72 째F).
