Sikkim:

Food:

Rice is a staple food, and fermented foods traditionally constitute a significant portion of the cuisine.
Nepalese cuisine is popular, as Sikkim is the only state of India
with an ethnic Nepali majority.

Vegetation:

Sikkim is home to around 5,000 species of flowering plants, 515 rare orchids,
60 primula species, 36 rhododendron species, 11 oak varieties, 23 bamboo varieties,
16 conifer species, 362 types of ferns and ferns allies, 8 tree ferns,
and over 900 medicinal plants.

Climate:

Sikkim's climate ranges from sub-tropical in the south to tundra in the north.
Most of the inhabited regions of Sikkim experience a temperate climate,
with temperatures seldom exceeding 28 째C (82 째F) in summer.
The average annual temperature for most of Sikkim
is around 18 째C (64 째F).
